\# 🚀 AI Business Growth Assistant



AI Business Growth Assistant is a full-stack web application that helps small businesses manage sales, expenses, revenue, and get intelligent business growth suggestions.



The application provides a dashboard to analyze business performance and make better decisions using AI-based recommendations.



\---



\## ✨ Features



\## 🔐 Authentication

\- User Registration

\- User Login

\- JWT Authentication

\- Secure Protected Routes

\- Logout Functionality



\---



\## 📊 Dashboard



\- Total Sales Tracking

\- Total Expense Tracking

\- Net Profit Calculation

\- Products Sold Count

\- Revenue Visualization

\- Expense Distribution Chart



\---



\## 💰 Sales Management



\- Add new sales

\- Store sales details

\- View sales records

\- Calculate total revenue



\---



\## 💳 Expense Management



\- Add business expenses

\- Categorize expenses

\- Track spending

\- View expense history



\---



\## 🤖 AI Business Suggestions



The system analyzes business data and provides suggestions like:



\- Sales improvement recommendations

\- Expense management advice

\- Business growth ideas

\- Performance analysis



\---



\## 📈 Reports



Business reports include:



\- Total Revenue

\- Total Expenses

\- Net Profit

\- Products Sold Summary



\---



\## 👤 Profile Management



\- View user details

\- Display account information

\- Logout option
